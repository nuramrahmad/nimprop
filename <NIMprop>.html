About NiMPROP

The Nigeria Mass Reduction of Out-of-School Children and Youth Project (NiMPROP) is dedicated to transforming lives through education and skill development. Our mission is to mop out 14 million children and youth from the streets and enroll them in accelerated learning and skill development programs within 4 years.

We aim to uplift more than 16 million Nigerian youth out of poverty, making them productive members of society by:

  • Providing education and skills acquisition
  • Reducing poverty and unemployment
  • Increasing productivity across the nation

Our Strategic Approach

Accelerated Education Systems

We design specialized educational programs to bridge learning gaps and facilitate rapid catch-up for out-of-school children, integrating skill-based learning for future employment.

Tailored Schooling Programs

Our initiatives address the unique challenges faced by marginalized groups and nomadic populations, ensuring inclusive education through mobile and adaptable learning structures.

Our Impact

NiMPROP is making a significant difference in Nigerian society:

  • Addressing educational disparities and empowering marginalized youth
  • Alleviating poverty through targeted interventions
  • Fostering peacebuilding initiatives and community cohesion
  • Laying the foundation for inclusive growth and collective prosperity

The Challenge We Face

Every child deserves education and care. The Almajiri system, while rooted in tradition, often leaves children vulnerable. We're working to ensure these children have access to both traditional and modern education, helping them build a brighter future.
